Bem-vindo ao Thunderbird

Porque precisamos de doações

O Thunderbird não é mais financiado pela Mozilla. Felizmente há uma comunidade ativa a mantê-lo funcionando e a desenvolvê-lo ainda mais. Mas para sobreviver a longo prazo, o projeto precisa de financiamento.

Thunderbird’s funding goes toward the significant infrastructure and staff that must be maintained to deliver the application to our tens of millions of users. For Thunderbird to survive and continue to evolve, we need your support and ask for your donation today. All the money donated will go directly to funding Thunderbird development and infrastructure.

Donate to Thunderbird

Thunderbird is the leading open source, cross-platform and calendaring client, free for business and personal use. We want it to stay secure and become even better. A donation will allow us to hire developers, pay for infrastructure, and continue to improve.

Thunderbird is funded by users like you! If you like Thunderbird, please consider making a donation. The best way for you to ensure Thunderbird remains available is to make a donation.

Envolva-se

O Thunderbird é um projeto de código aberto, o que significa que qualquer um pode contribuir com ideias, código e tempo ajudando outros usuários. Torne-se parte de uma equipe que dá suporte aos usuários do Thunderbird e faça do Thunderbird um programa sensacional. Junte-se a nós.

Obtenha suporte

Need help with something in Thunderbird? We have a support page where you can ask questions and read knowledge base articles to learn how to use Thunderbird.

Relate um problema

Think you’ve found a problem with Thunderbird? Create a bug report so that we can make sure it gets solved. Helping us identify and fix bugs makes for a better Thunderbird for all.
