Anṣuf ar Thunderbird

Nesra tawsa

Tezriḍ ahat d akken asnerni n Thunderbird ur yezzi terfed-it Mozilla. Acukan, tamezdagnut turmiṭ tettkemmil taneflit ines. Ihi, akken asenfaṛ ad yidir ugar, yesra tadrimt.

Thunderbird’s funding goes toward the significant infrastructure and staff that must be maintained to deliver the application to our tens of millions of users. For Thunderbird to survive and continue to evolve, we need your support and ask for your donation today. All the money donated will go directly to funding Thunderbird development and infrastructure.

Mudd tawsa i Thunderbird

Thunderbird is the leading open source, cross-platform email and calendaring client, free for business and personal use. We want it to stay secure and become even better. A donation will allow us to hire developers, pay for infrastructure, and continue to improve.

Asnerni n Thunderbird refdent iseqdacen am kunwi! Ma tḥemlem Thunderbird, ma ulac aɣilif walit ma tzemrem ad muddem tawsa.Abrid ifazen akken ad yili Thunderbird d amezgu d amuddu n tawsa.

Ttekki

Thunderbird d asenfar n uɣbalu yeldin, aya yebɣa ad d-yini belli yal yiwen izmer ad ittekki s tiktiwin, s isental, s tangalt akk s wakud akken ad d-yefk afus i yiseqdacen nniḍen. Uɣal d yiwen ger iɛeggalen n tarbaɛt yesnernayen Thunderbird. rnu-d ɣur-neɣ

Awi tallalt

Tesriḍ tallalt d kra n tɣawsa deg Thunderbird? Nesɛa asebter n tallalt anida tzemreḍ ad testeqsiḍ neɣ ad teɣreḍ imagraden ɣef useqdec n Thunderbird.

Ineqqisen n ibugen

Tɣileḍ tufiḍ ugur deg Thunderbird? Rnu aneqqis n ubug akken ad neḍmen ad ifru. Amuddu n ufus deg usulu akked ferru n ibugen ittɛawan asnerni n Thunderbird i iseqdacen meṛṛa.
