Contribute to Thunderbird

To ensure that Thunderbird continues to improve, as it has for the last ten years, active contributors recently organized to focus on the future. Now is a great time for you to get involved. Please help us assist users of Thunderbird, and improve the Thunderbird product and eco-system.

We look forward to hearing from you.
